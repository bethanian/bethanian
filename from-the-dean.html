From the Dean of Academics

Welcome to the academic heart of Southern Bethany Christian School of Minglanilla, Inc. As Dean of Academics, I am privileged to oversee our institution's commitment to educational excellence and spiritual growth.

Our academic program is designed to challenge, inspire, and empower students to reach their full potential. We believe in fostering critical thinking, creativity, and character development through a comprehensive curriculum that integrates Christian values with modern educational practices.

Together with our dedicated faculty, we strive to create an environment where students can explore their interests, develop their talents, and prepare for the challenges of higher education and beyond.

Dr. Maria Santos
Dean of Academics
