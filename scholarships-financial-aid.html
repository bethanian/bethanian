Financial Aid Process

  1. Complete FAFSA

    Fill out the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) form.

  2. Submit Required Documents

    Provide income statements, tax returns, and other necessary documentation.

  3. Review Aid Package

    Meet with our financial aid counselors to review your aid package.

  4. Accept Aid Offer

    Accept your financial aid offer and complete any additional requirements.
